Grillo

The grilids are a family of orthopteran insects of the superfamily Grylloidea, within the suborder Ensifera. To this family belong insects commonly known as crickets. They are usually brown to black insects with nocturnal habits. Some species are found in houses, domestic crickets, for example Acheta domesticus and Gryllus bimaculatus in temperate zones and Gryllodes supplicans or Gryllodes sigillatus in tropical areas.

Midges

Midge, (family Chironomidae), also called chironomid, nonbiting midge, or gnat, any of a group of tiny two-winged flies (order Diptera) that superficially resemble mosquitoesA midge is any small fly, including species in several families of non-mosquito Nematoceran Diptera. Midges are found (seasonally or otherwise) on practically every land area outside permanently arid deserts and the frigid zones..
